Who Are These People?

Denver Illiterati is a social book club based in (you guessed it) Denver, Colorado. We usually meet the first Monday of the month to discuss our current book, reveal our next book selection, and generally hang out and be awesome. Meetings typically take place in-or-around Denver's Highlands neighborhood, either at comfy businesses or at our members' homes.

We rotate which member picks the next book, and have read across many genres of fiction and non-fiction.

Joining is easy. Just show up. That's it. If you've read the book, or read a book, even better!
